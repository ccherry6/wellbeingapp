## Step 9: Upload Build

### From Xcode:
1. Select **Any iOS Device** as the build destination
2. Go to **Product** → **Archive**
3. Once archived, click **Distribute App**
4. Choose **App Store Connect**
5. Choose **Upload**
6. Follow prompts to sign and upload

### Typical Upload Time:
- 10-30 minutes to upload
- 10-30 minutes to process in App Store Connect
- Once processed, you can select it for your app submission

---

## Step 10: Submit for Review

1. In App Store Connect, go to your app
2. Click **+ Version or Platform** → **iOS**
3. Enter version number: **1.0**
4. Fill in all required fields (use info above)
5. Select your uploaded build
6. Click **Add for Review**
7. Click **Submit to App Review**

---

## Important Notes

1. **First submission takes longer** - Apple reviews new apps more carefully
2. **Rejections are common** - Don't worry, you can resubmit with fixes
3. **Common rejection reasons:**
   - Missing test account credentials
   - Crashes or bugs
   - Incomplete functionality
   - Privacy policy issues
   - Misleading screenshots or description

4. **You need test accounts** - Create these BEFORE starting submission:
   - reviewer@test.com (student role)
   - coach@test.com (coach role)
