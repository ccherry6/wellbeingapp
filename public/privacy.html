Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2024

1. Introduction

Thrive Wellbeing ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of student wellbeing data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our wellbeing monitoring platform.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name and email address
  • Student ID number
  • Sport and program year
  • Contact preferences

Wellbeing Data:

  • Daily wellness questionnaire responses (sleep quality, energy levels, mood, stress, etc.)
  • Training fatigue and muscle soreness levels
  • Academic pressure indicators
  • Relationship satisfaction
  • Program belonging
  • Optional biometric data (HRV, resting heart rate) if provided
  • Injury and illness status
  • Free-text notes you choose to provide
  • Goals and progress tracking

Technical Information:

  • Login timestamps and session data
  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address (for security purposes)
  • App usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Wellbeing Support: To monitor your daily wellness and provide timely support when needed
  • Early Intervention: To identify students who may need additional support and enable coaches to reach out
  • Trend Analysis: To help you and your coaches understand patterns in your wellbeing over time
  • Program Improvement: To improve our support services and wellbeing programs
  • Research: Anonymized data may be used for research purposes (only with your explicit consent)
  • Communication: To send you wellness reminders and important notifications

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Who Can See Your Data:

  • You: You have full access to all your wellness data
  • BDC Coaches: Authorized coaching staff can view your wellness entries to provide support
  • BDC Administration: Designated administrators can access aggregated data and individual records when duty of care is required

We DO NOT:

  • Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Use your data for purposes other than wellbeing support and program improvement
  • Share identifiable information with external researchers without explicit consent

Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or in cases where we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of students or others.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Hosting: Data is hosted on Supabase, a secure cloud database provider
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Access Control: Row Level Security (RLS) ensures only authorized users can access specific data
  • Authentication: Secure login with password encryption
  • Backup: Regular automated backups to prevent data loss
  • Audit Logs: All data access is logged for security monitoring

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Wellness Entries: Kept for the duration of your enrollment plus 2 years for longitudinal analysis
  • Account Information: Retained while you are actively using the platform
  • Research Data: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for research purposes (with consent)
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data at any time

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (within 24 hours of submission)
  • Export: Download your wellness data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of research data sharing at any time
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing (may affect service provision)

8. Research Data Use

If you consent to research participation:

  • Your data will be assigned a research code to protect your identity
  • No personally identifiable information will be included in research outputs
  • Aggregate data may be published in academic papers or reports
  • You can withdraw consent at any time
  • Withdrawal will not affect your use of the platform

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies and local storage to:

  • Keep you logged in securely
  • Remember your preferences
  • Ensure the app functions properly

We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for students aged 16 and older. We comply with applicable privacy laws regarding minors. If we become aware that we have collected data from anyone under the age of 16 without proper consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Email notification to your registered email address
  • In-app notification
  • Posting the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date

12. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication (Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy)
  • Netlify/Hosting Provider: Website hosting

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures in place.
