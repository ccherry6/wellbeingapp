Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I access the BDC Thrive platform?

Web: Visit thrivewellbeing.me in any web browser

iOS App: Download "BDC Thrive" from the Apple App Store

First time? Use the QR code provided by your coach to register quickly.

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact us.

Q: How often should I complete the wellness questionnaire?

We recommend completing the questionnaire daily, ideally in the morning. This helps you and your coaches track patterns and identify when you might need support. You can enable daily reminders in the app settings.

Q: Who can see my wellness data?

Your data is visible to:

  • You (full access to all your data)
  • BDC coaching staff (to provide support and monitor wellbeing)
  • BDC administrators (for duty of care purposes)

Your data is never shared with external parties without your explicit consent. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: Can I delete or edit my wellness entries?

Within 24 hours: You can delete entries to correct mistakes.

After 24 hours: Entries cannot be deleted to maintain data integrity, but you can contact us if you need to correct important information.

Editing: You can edit most fields in your entries at any time.

Q: What happens if I select "I want to speak to someone"?

When you indicate you want to speak to someone:

  • Your coach receives an immediate alert
  • They will reach out to you as soon as possible (typically within 24 hours)
  • You can specify who you'd prefer to speak with
  • All conversations remain confidential
Q: Is my data secure and private?

Yes. We use industry-standard security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication with password protection
  • Access controls ensure only authorized staff can view your data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with Australian privacy laws
Q: Can I use the app offline?

iOS App: Limited offline functionality. You can view previously loaded data offline.

Web: Requires internet connection for most features.

Note: Submitting wellness entries requires an internet connection to sync with the database.

Q: How do I enable push notifications?

iOS:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Scroll to "BDC Thrive"
  3. Tap "Notifications"
  4. Enable "Allow Notifications"

In-App: Go to Settings > Notifications and customize your reminder preferences.

Q: What should I do if the app isn't working properly?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Close and reopen the app
  3. Update to the latest version (App Store)
  4. Restart your device
  5. Clear browser cache (web version)

If problems persist, contact us with details about the issue, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Description of the problem
  • Screenshots if possible
Q: Can I export my wellness data?

Yes. You can export your data in several ways:

  • PDF Reports: Generate and download visual reports from your dashboard
  • Data Export: Request a full data export by contacting us
  • Screenshots: Use the built-in screenshot feature for your trends
Q: How do I opt out of research data collection?

You can manage your research consent in Settings:

  1. Log in to BDC Thrive
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Find "Research Participation"
  4. Toggle off research consent

This will not affect your use of the platform or the support you receive.

Q: What if I'm having technical issues with QR code login?

QR code troubleshooting:

  • Ensure you're using a device with a camera
  • Allow camera permissions when prompted
  • Make sure the QR code is clearly visible and well-lit
  • Try using the manual registration option instead
  • Contact your coach for a new QR code if needed
